Email Settings—you can change your settings for email notifications through the IHS Connect Daily Summary on this page.


My Saved—any content you save on Connect can be found on My Saved with the option to set Email Alerts for immediate, daily, or weekly notification of updates to the content. The content can include documents, charts, series, queries, dashboards, or scenarios. Each content area of Connect (Home, Insight, Industry, Geography, Company) and Tools contains research and data that you may want to save for later review. Opportunities to save content appear throughout Connect in the Add to My Connect feature.


Service Alerts—these service-based email alerts can be changed on the Service Alerts tab for immediate, immediate with attachment, daily, or weekly alerts about added and updated content to your subscription-based services.


Dashboard—the Dashboard page provides a workspace where you can design your workflow and dig deeper into the details of research for Country Risk, Economics, Energy, and Chemical domains on IHS Connect.
