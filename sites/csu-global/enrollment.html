Colorado State University Pueblo Online was created for transfer students who have already completed credits towards a degree.

We will evaluate the credits you have completed at other institutions and do everything we can to allow you to use those credits towards your CSU Pueblo degree. Up to 90 credits are transferable from accredited universities and colleges.

Contact an Enrollment Counselor today to find out how many credits you have already completed towards a Bachelor's degree in Construction Management.

Admissions Requirements:

  • Students must have at least 12 transferrable credits and preferably an associate's degree in a similar field. Students must complete their general education requirements prior to being admitted to this program.

  • Students should have a cumulative GPA of 2.3 or above. Students with a GPA between 1.8 and 2.3 may also be admitted after committee review.

    • How to Apply:

  • Step 1: Talk to an Enrollment Counselor. Our highly trained staff is available to answer all of your questions, and help you select the program that fits your goals.

  • Step 2: Apply. Complete the online application and pay the $25 application fee when required.

  • Step 3: Submit your transcripts. Order your official transcripts from your previous colleges and other sources of credit, so we can create your individual degree plan. Please have your previous institutions mail your official transcripts to:

    • CSU Pueblo Online
    7800 East Orchard Road, Suite 200 Greenwood Village, CO 80111
    For electronic transcripts, please have them emailed to admissions@csupuebloonline.co

  • Step 4: Finalize your enrollment. Once you are admitted, our Student Support team will guide you through orientation, and also help you to understand your degree plan, transfer credits, and financing options.

  • Step 5: Start learning! The only thing left is for you to order your textbooks and get ready to start class.
