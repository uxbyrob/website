Accordion Guidelines

Size and Layout

Content

- Main purpose of accordion is to encompass lists, not read content, like articles, etc.

- Content will not expand into summaries/bylines like our article modules, no attachment icons

- Links are clicked on to visit another page, tool or expanded module

- One exception to above guideline is a search picker can be included (company,country,etc)

- Recommended presentation of content is a top down priority. Most important to least

- BA team is working a priority template to be used, includes an elective to push one section up in priority

- Graphs/Tables/Visualizations can be placed in a accordion panel,must fit to size restraint

- If article type content is listed, no more than 10 article titles will show


Function/Feature

- top panel is open by default

- no other panels are open on load

- panels can be expanded ala carte,

- Each panel section has a header panel that can be clicked on to expand

- Expanding panel must always have a header panel.

- Links on hover state, underline

- 320px

- Header heights are single line and a height of 32px

- Container panels can expand height wise as much as they need

- No content is recommended to put below the accordion container, as it will be hidden from view of user, exception would be marketing material/ads

- Marketing material can be put above the accordion container (TBD)
