CPL3 Communications Archive

Miss something? Check our communications archive.

We want you to have all the information needed to use the CPL3 and make sound decisions for your projects. We're doing our best to make our plans and intent available well before they impact you.

If we record a meeting or give other information that needs to be made available well after the fact we'll post it here. We're just getting started so please let us know what we can do better.

Media, videos, audio

There are some quirks when working with WebEx recordings. When it makes sense we'll likely record our meetings and post them here. If the meeting started out as a WebEx meeting we receive the WebEx recording as an ".arf" file which isn't very usefull as few people have an updated player installed that can play them. So, we tend to transcode them to ".mp4" files to make them available for download and general viewing.

Tip: We recommend downloading our media files and playing them in a player like VLC Media Player that can ramp up the playback speed to make the most of your time.
