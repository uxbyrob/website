"Walk in the user's shoes! I deep dive into a user's experience and provide feedback to all invested stakeholders and team members. I dig to understand, empathize and analyze, via user interviews, surveys, research, A/B testing, lab testing or user groups. I study user tasks, shadow flows and understand behaviors. I strive to find the pain points, misses and avoidable friction when using a product. Alongside those findings, I aim to discover will this feature request benefit existing customers and attract new ones. Will this item generate new sales and income? Are these findings, competitive?
