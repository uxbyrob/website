Chemical needs a feature to their users that via ads can recommend or solicit other available products for their business line in Connect. This can also flood to other business lines.



These ads need to be non evasive and provide option to dismiss.


UX team recommends the approach of presenting a promotion that will hide after a determined about of time (30 seconds?). While hidden user can still click show/expand icon to display the promotion spot again. Once user shows no inactivity on that spot, if will hide again after 6 seconds (TBD). The promotion can present itself in two states, a small height banner and then a larger if needed to show more information.
