CSU-Pueblo Online understands the financial commitment it takes to pay for your undergraduate degree. We work with our students through a variety of payment options to find one that works best for you.

Financial Aid

At this time, CSU Pueblo Online does not offer Financial Aid for its programs, but this option will be coming soon. Ask your Enrollment Counselor for more information.

Cash Payment

For our students who want to handle paying their tuition on their own, we make it easy by allowing you to pay your tuition in two parts: you pay 50% of your tuition by the first day of class and the remaining 50% of your tuition 30 days into the term using one of the accepted payment methods. You may update your preferred payment method at any time, but students who do not have another financing method selected will automatically be cash pay students.

Pay Online Options

All CSU-Pueblo Online tuition and fee charges may be paid online through the Student Portal using the following methods:

  • MasterCard
  • Visa
  • Discover
  • American Express
  • Electronic payment from checking/savings account
  • (CSU-Pueblo Online accepts debit cards but is not authorized for automatic deductions).

Pay by Check or Money Order

Students may also pay by check, money order, or cash. Check or money order payments by mail must be received by the due date.

Make checks and money orders payable to:
CSU Pueblo Online

and mailed to:

Colorado State University-Pueblo Online
Attention: Student Accounts
7800 East Orchard Road, Suite 200
Greenwood Village, Colorado 80111

Employer Reimbursement Tuition Deferment

If your employer offers Tuition Reimbursement and you meet the qualifications, CSU Pueblo Online will defer your tuition payment for 30 days after your term ends. Talk to your company's Human Resources department to ensure you qualify.

Military Benefits

A variety of military benefits are available to current military, their spouses and dependents, and Veterans as part of CSU Pueblo Online's commitment to our armed services.
