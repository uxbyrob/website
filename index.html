"Walk in the user's shoes!" I lead and evangelize the importance of research with team focus to deep dive into user experiences and to present and provide that feedback to all invested stakeholders and team members. I dig for and coach designers to better understand, empathize and analyze the needs/wants of the user. Find the root problem to solve and not just the symptoms! Where are gaps in product features, identified pain points, cognitive load and difficulties to complete tasks. Where are opportunities to innovate and enhance the product? What can be contributed or removed from the UX roadmap.

Regular means I lean on and incorporate for research and testing are user interviews, surveys, A/B sampling, lab testing, data discovery, online usability sites and user groups. I study tasks, shadow workflows and yearn to understand behaviors thoroughly. I strive to find the pain points, misses and friction when using a product. Where does the product fall short unknowingly? Where are the valued features to focus on and enhance? In research did aha moments surface?
