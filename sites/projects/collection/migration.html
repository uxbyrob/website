SAME DAY ANALYSIS

Analysis of Articles and commentary for specific to their industry and industry heads-up. Segmented by Region and service. Features RSS and Mobile feeds. Links to “Article view”. Displayed by day with ability to go to previous days.


BULLETINS

POLI Bulletin - Monthly basis, users are notified on landing page or via email of updates to service bulletins. There are other service bulletins. Bulletins relate to our services offered. Smaller information then special  reports.


SPECIAL REPORTS

Global and very detailed reports for industry, or a drug, etc. Can be filtered by Region and country, Has RSS and Mobile Feed. Links to “Article View”. Articles can be exported *


COUNTRY SUMMARY

Much like Country profile in Connect.

- overview, outlook, infrastructure, demographics, expenditure, policy, regulatory system, p&r system, pharma market. Can set notification/alerts for each of these.. ** Strange export section, has one for full report and one for sections

- has related/latest articles at bottom of country profile page


SEARCH / —> ONCOLOGY DATABASE

Users use search to find prices, will most of the time search a drug directly. Search listing of prices, color means we estimated prices, no colors were used directly from source. Users can export to spreadsheet pricing info and spreadsheet. take note of some of the spreadsheets have combined data, rather then separated. Before export users can choose what currency to export.


Results allows user to see a summary table and then click to see single page views.


Oncology Database provides more information, such as therapy costs, exchange rates, amount of use, directions for drug that determine lifecycle with a patient.


MARKET ACCESS RISK SCORE

Calculates a score for each customer, to gauge how challenging it is too launch a product on the market. Has filters and ability to view summary or detail. Filters include country, risk factors.


WORLD MARKET HEALTHCARE

Still learning what this is exactly in comparison to country profile. This does break down by country and features a SWOT table, country profile matrix, litigation highlights and Biosimilars pipeline database, all with their own searches


REFERENCE PRICING MATRIX

Tool allows a comparison of countries for pricing to gauge the influence of a country on another for pricing. This is per each pharmaceutical/drug.


PRICING AND REIMBURSEMENT

Features P&R Policy database, reference pricing matrix, pharmonline international (old and new), risk and sharing database


RISK SHARING DATABASE

Gauge risk of a drug that is on the market, how effective, how it’s performing.
